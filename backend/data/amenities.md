# Greenfield Apartments - Amenities & Building Guide

## Building Amenities

### Fitness Center
Open 24/7 for all residents. Features treadmills, ellipticals, free weights, squat rack, cable machines, and a stretching area. Towel service is available. Personal training sessions can be arranged through the front desk.

### Rooftop Lounge
Open daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Features comfortable seating, BBQ grills (4 available), a fire pit, and panoramic city views. The rooftop can be reserved for private events for a $200 rental fee — contact the leasing office to book.

### Package Room
24/7 access with secure smart lockers. You'll receive a text notification with a unique code when a package arrives. Oversized packages are held at the front desk during office hours (Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM, Sat 10AM-4PM).

### Bike Storage
Secure indoor bike storage room on the ground floor. Each resident gets one assigned bike space included in rent. Additional spaces are $25/month. Bike repair station with basic tools is available.

### Laundry
All 1-bedroom and larger units come with in-unit washer/dryer. Studio units have access to the shared laundry room on the 2nd floor ($2.00 wash, $1.50 dry, card-operated).

### Business Center
Located on the 1st floor, open 24/7. Features a printer/scanner, two semi-private workstations, and a small meeting room (bookable via the tenant portal, max 2 hours per reservation).

### Pool
Seasonal outdoor pool open Memorial Day through Labor Day, 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Maximum capacity of 30 people. Pool furniture available first-come first-served. No glass containers permitted in the pool area.

## Building Rules Quick Reference

- **Trash & Recycling**: Trash chute on each floor. Recycling room in basement. Large item disposal must be arranged with management (fee may apply).
- **Hallways**: Keep hallways clear of personal items (shoes, mats, deliveries). Items left in hallways will be removed after 24 hours notice.
- **Balconies**: No grills on balconies (use rooftop BBQs instead). No hanging items over railings. Plants and furniture are welcome.
- **Elevator**: Please be mindful during peak hours. Moving/large deliveries require elevator reservation.
- **Front Door**: Building entry requires key fob. Do not hold the door for unknown individuals. Report lost fobs immediately ($25 replacement fee).
