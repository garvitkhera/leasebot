# Greenfield Apartments - Standard Lease Agreement

## Terms and Conditions

### Lease Duration
All leases are for a minimum of 12 months. Month-to-month leases are available after the initial 12-month term at a 10% premium on the base rent. Early termination requires 60 days written notice and a fee equal to 2 months' rent.

### Rent Payment
Rent is due on the 1st of each month. A grace period of 5 days is provided. Late fees of $50 apply after the 5th of the month, with an additional $10 per day thereafter. Rent can be paid via the online tenant portal, check, or ACH transfer.

### Security Deposit
A security deposit equal to one month's rent is required at lease signing. The deposit is refundable within 30 days of move-out, minus any deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear. An itemized list of deductions will be provided.

### Pet Policy
Cats and small dogs (under 35 lbs) are welcome with a one-time pet deposit of $300 and monthly pet rent of $35 per pet. Maximum 2 pets per unit. Breed restrictions apply — please contact the leasing office for the full list. All pets must be registered with the management office. Emotional support animals are exempt from pet fees with proper documentation.

### Parking
Underground garage parking is available at $150/month per space. Surface lot parking is $75/month. Each unit is entitled to a maximum of 2 parking spaces. Visitor parking is available on a first-come basis in designated spots.

### Utilities
Water, sewer, and trash are included in rent. Tenants are responsible for electricity, gas, and internet. Average monthly utility costs are approximately $80-$120 for a 1-bedroom and $120-$180 for a 2-bedroom unit.

### Maintenance and Repairs
Tenants should submit maintenance requests through the online portal or by contacting the leasing office. Emergency maintenance (flooding, no heat, gas leak) has a 24/7 emergency line. Non-emergency requests are addressed within 48 business hours. Tenants are responsible for minor maintenance such as replacing light bulbs, air filters (provided quarterly), and keeping drains clear.

### Move-In / Move-Out
Move-ins and move-outs must be scheduled with the management office at least 7 days in advance. Elevator reservations for moving are required. A move-in/move-out checklist will be completed jointly with management to document the unit condition. Professional cleaning of carpets is required at move-out.

### Guest Policy
Guests may stay for up to 14 consecutive days. Stays longer than 14 days require written approval from management and may be subject to additional occupancy charges. All overnight guests must be registered at the front desk.

### Noise Policy
Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM daily. Music, television, and other noise should be kept at a reasonable level at all times. Repeated noise complaints may result in lease violation notices.

### Renters Insurance
All tenants are required to maintain renters insurance with a minimum coverage of $100,000 in personal liability. Proof of insurance must be provided at move-in and annually thereafter. Greenfield Apartments must be listed as an interested party on the policy.

### Smoking Policy
Greenfield Apartments is a smoke-free community. Smoking (including e-cigarettes and vaping) is prohibited in all indoor areas including units, hallways, and common areas. Designated outdoor smoking areas are available on the ground floor patio.
